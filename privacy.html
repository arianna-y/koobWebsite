Privacy Policy for Koob

At Koob, we are committed to protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share data, including email addresses and phone interaction data, when you use our mobile eBook reader app. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Data Collection

We may collect the following types of data when you use the App:
    Personal Information: We will collect your email address, when you create an account or register with the App.
    Phone Interaction Data: We may collect data related to your phone interactions with Koob including how long a person spent on a page, and which words they look up. Voteword, wordlookup, and how long spent and which book they choose. Data sharing book specific.

Data Use

We may use the data collected for the following purposes:
    To provide and improve the App: We may use your personal information and phone interaction data to provide and improve the functionality, features, and performance of the App.
    To personalize your experience: We may use your data to personalize your experience within the App, such as recommending books based on your reading habits and preferences.
    To communicate with you: We may use your personal information to send you updates, notifications, and other communications related to the App.
    To conduct research and analytics: We may use analytics tools to analyze and understand how users interact with the App, to improve our services and for other research purposes.

Data Sharing

We may share your data with the following parties:
    Service Providers: We may share your data with third-party service providers who help us operate, maintain, and improve the App. These service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not allowed to use your data for any other purposes.
    Legal Requirements: We may share your data if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request, such as a court order or subpoena.
    Business Transfers: If our business is acquired or merged with another company, your data may be transferred to the new owners as part of the transaction.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Data Deletion

We store data until it is no longer necessary to provide our services or until your account is deleted - whichever comes first. This is a case-by-case determination that depends on things like the nature of the data, why it is collected and processed, and relevant legal or operational retention needs.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on https://yuanarianna.wixsite.com/koob or by sending you a notification. Your continued use of the App after any changes to the Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at koob.gator@gmail.com
