Learning Page

The learning activity tracking functionality allows users to visit past history and easily revise looked-up words.

Word Look-up History

Review challenging words, and quickly look up the word without leaving the reading application.

Users can view a slideshow of pictures representing the meaning of the word and listen to the audio of the looked-up word.

Easy Reading

The user can review the number of times the word was searched and the frequency, as well as view activities such as the time spent reading a page of the book.

Definitions Voting

Context is vital for understanding the meaning of words, so the same words that appear in two different sentences may be different.

Students can vote which meaning best describes the word, considering the context.

Users can also view the AI-predicted context definition.

Reading Statistics

Gain insight to the progress of learning. User activity is shown in the form of a dynamic and interactive graphical user interface.

Teacher's View

Users logged in as teachers have access to specific functionalities. These include adding custom definitions for the words that seem vague, view the reading statistics of the students' reading, see the time spent per page on the book by each student, and more.

Review Definitions

Review definitions of the words that have been searched and the frequency of each word searched. This provides insight for users to learn and grow.
