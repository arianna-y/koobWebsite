About Koob

Koob is an eBook reader that reads ePUB-formatted books. The goal of Koob is to help people read and learn English, and to provide
an enhanced reading and learning experience for ePUB readers with features such as pictorial word lookup, AI vote, and teacher/student
vote for the meaning of looked-up words based on the context. Users can look for teacher votes, AI votes, and other student votes to get
a better sense of the context. We will launch a user study to evaluate our hypothesis of the usability or impact of Koob on English learners.
